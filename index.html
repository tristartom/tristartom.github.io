Research Interests

My mission is to understand, enable, and verify the security of digital infrastructures in high-impact and emerging domains. I focus on the research of cybersecurity, systems, and interdisciplinary topics in decentralized systems (e.g., blockchains and smart contracts), open-source ecosystems, and other emerging technologies, My research addresses technical challenges:

  • Security analysis and vulnerability discovery: [USENIX Security 24], [CCS 21]

  • Provable security and economics of security: [IEEE S&P 25]

  • Security-oriented measurement and cyber-crimes: [Euro S&P 23], [IMC 21], [NDSS 21]

  • Perf. optimization & workload analysis: [TSE 23/FSE 21], [Middleware 20], [ICDE 19], [TPDS 13]

  • Secure data storage: [ACSAC 14], [EDBT 14], [ICDCS 08]

  • Data privacy: [TKDE 15], [ICDCS 14], [CIKM 11]

  • Security/systems education: [BADD labs], [FuSSIL labs]

My research is generously supported by National Science Foundation (NSF), Ethereum Foundation, Flashbots, Intel, NSA, etc. My research results in patches in popular open-source software (e.g., [Geth 1.11.4]).
