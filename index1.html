Biography
I am a first-year Ph.D. student in College of Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology. I am now working with Prof Ling Liu. I have obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer Science, respectively in 2006 and 2009, both from Fudan University, Shanghai, China. My adviser at Fudan University is Prof Shuigeng Zhou.

My research interests are in distributed systems and databases, security and privacy, virtual machines, and cloud computing. Previously, I have worked on data management systems in peer-to-peer networks. More information can be found in my CV.
