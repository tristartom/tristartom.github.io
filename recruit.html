Computer Science Ph.D. (Spring & Fall, 2022) [Chinese version]

There are a few Ph.D. positions opening in Spring and Fall, 2022 in Prof. Tang's group in the EECS department of Syracuse University. The position will be supported fully by the Graduate Research Assistantship (GRA). Dr. Tang's research is broadly on cyber-security and distributed systems, with the current focus on blockchain systems. On the blockchain topic, his research group (https:tristartom.github.iomembers.html) has produced technical papers in top-tier cyber-security and systems conferences (including ACM CCS, NDSS, ACM IMC, ESECFSE, IEEE ICDE, ACM Middleware, etc). You can find details on his website: http:ecs.syr.edufacultyyuzhe .

Self-motivated students with strong skills in systems, programming and security tools are encouraged to apply. You are welcome to send an email to Dr. Tang (ytang100@syr.edu) to inquire about the positions. Please include your C.V. and transcript in the email. Note that the Spring, 2022 position will be open until filled by the first admitted applicant.
