Presentation Requirement

For making slides and doing presentation efficiently, here is a check list:

  • Focus on the intuition, get rid of formality (no formular!).
  • Include slide number!
  • Fewer slides; Make each slide a story!
  • Never copy&paste text from paper; tell the story in your own words!
  • Talk about things you do understand, ignore things you don't (and admit it when being asked about).
  • Follow the logic flow; make sure every word in a slide is explained before entering the slide!

For efficient paper reading, you might find this brochure useful: "Efficient reading of papers in Science and Technology" [pdf]
