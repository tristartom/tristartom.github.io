Presentation Requirement

Each presentation will last one hour and fifteen minutes; Your presentation should take about ONE hours and the other 15 minutes are left for QA and discussions.

For each reading program (i.e. one topic presentation), you should read the papers with ★ in depth, and read other papers briefly (e.g. on the Introduction part) to compare and contrast those with ★ .

In the presentation, you might want to focus on the intuition/idea of the paper, and feel free to ignore all the formality; for example, there will be no need to show slides full of formulas if they do not serve to illustrate the idea better.

For presenter, send me your slides prior to the day of the class. In addition, each presenter should also send me three phrases that best summarize each paper you read in depth; each phrase should not be more than three words.
